Leaving kids unsupervised?

At what age can a child be left alone?


Leagally: Various state by state.
In Washington state the recommended age is 10 years old


Most states leave it up to the parents to determine if the child is mature enough to be left home alone.

Minimum age of home alone child by state.

Here are a few guidelines on the subject.

  • Is the child mature enough to not cause cause havoc, chaos, and destruction?
  • Does your child have access to a telephone?
    1. Can your child destinquish between an emergency or question?
    2. Does the child know at least 2 adult phone numbers?
    3. Does your child know 911 and most importantly when to use 911?
  • Is your child mature enough to babysit a younger child?
  • Does your child know not to eat/drink unknown foods/liquids?
  • Can your child be mentally capable of not being tricked into opening the door for strangers?

These are questions a parent/guardian must ask themselves before leaving a child home alone. Can your child answer yes to all these questions?
